Continuing on the topic of strings, this universe provides you with all sorts of ways to manipulate them.

For example, you've asked a peddler how much gold a fistfull of dates would cost. He replies:

"THOSE DATES WILL COST 190 GOLD!"

Well, there's no reason to shout. With a function called 'toLowerCase()' we can calm him down.

This function can be called by 'chaining' it onto the variable itself: variableName.toLowerCase();
